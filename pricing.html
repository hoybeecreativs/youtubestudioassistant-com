Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Free Trial work?

The Free Trial is completely free forever with ALL features included. You can use it to test the extension and see if it fits your workflow. No credit card required, no limitations!

What's the difference between Free and Lifetime?

Both plans have identical features! The Lifetime plan is a way to support our development with a small one-time payment of $5. You get the same unlimited features either way.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and various local payment methods through our secure payment processor.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for the Lifetime plan. If you're not satisfied, contact us at support@youtubestudioassistant.com for a full refund.

Do I need to provide my own API keys?

For the Free Trial with Gemini AI, no API key is needed. For Lifetime Access with OpenAI or DeepSeek, you'll need to provide your own API keys for those specific providers.

Why offer both Free and Lifetime if they're the same?

We believe in making our tools accessible to everyone. The Free plan ensures anyone can use all features. The Lifetime plan is for those who want to support our development and help us keep improving the extension!
