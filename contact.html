Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I install the extension?

Visit the Chrome Web Store, search for "YouTube Studio Assistant", and click "Add to Chrome". Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Is it really free?

Yes! We offer a free 7-day trial with full access to all features. After the trial, you can purchase a lifetime license.

Which AI providers are supported?

We support Google Gemini, OpenAI (GPT-4), and DeepSeek. You can use your own API keys or choose from our supported providers.

How many languages are supported?

YouTube Studio Assistant supports translation to 110+ languages, covering virtually every major language worldwide.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We don't store your video content or API keys on our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Can I use it for multiple channels?

Yes! One license works across all your YouTube channels. There are no limits on the number of channels or videos.
